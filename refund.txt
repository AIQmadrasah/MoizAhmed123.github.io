Refund Policy

At Assalaam Institute of Quraan Foundation, we prioritize your satisfaction and strive to provide products and services of the highest quality. However, we understand that circumstances may arise where a refund is necessary. Please review our refund policy below.

EVENT TICKETS

Refunds for event tickets are available up to 7 days before the event date. After this period, tickets are non-refundable, but they may be transferable to another individual upon request.

COACHING AND CONSULTING SERVICES

Refunds for coaching and consulting services are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you are unsatisfied with the service provided, please contact us to discuss your concerns and potential options for a refund.


HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at assalaamiq@gmail.com with your order details and reason for the refund request. We will process your refund as quickly as possible, typically within 5-7 business days.
