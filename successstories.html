Success Stories

“In April 2018 I joined this Madrasah and what I can say about the Madrasah is that the environment I was around was built in motivation for success. The teachers in the Madrasah were always looking for ways to help the students succeed and that is the one thing I really enjoyed about being in this Madrasah. They always put the students ahead of everything and looked for every possible way to make them not only strive in their learning of Hifz, but also do the best in making them role models. I am grateful for the teachers who took their time with me and helped manage my time in completing Hifz. For those who read this may Allah ﷻ make it easy for you and keep you steadfast in your journey of memorizing the Quran.” - Hafiz Sabri Faiz, 18 years old

I spent over a span of two years in AIQ. The teachers are friendly yet firm. They pay attention to every student and know their weaknesses and strengths. The whole day is planned so that students finish all their assigned lessons. I am blessed to be a part of a Madrasah where the teachers are our real guide both in this life and the hereafter. We feel free to discuss anything from sports to current affairs with our Maulana. They keep our manners and Akhlaaq under check. For our teachers believe in complete development as an exemplary Muslim. Their dedication and love for each student is evident when they are ready to help at any time and give us extra time also. From the first Hafiz to the latest we are still in some way or the other connected to the Madrasah. I am blessed and thank Allah ﷻ for choosing me to be part of this institution." - Hafiz Abdur Rahim Khan, 13 years old

“I have completed my Hifz in this Madrasah. Ever since I joined this Madrasah, my performance has improved tremendously. The environment is friendly; the teachers give lots of attention to all the students, which allow them to work to their full potential. This Madrasah also emphasizes on having good manners and following the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.” - Hafiz Zakir Muhammad, 14 years old

I have been learning with this madrassah since 2015 and Alhamdulillah it has helped in a great way.The madrassah has a great learning and environment which made the memorization of the holy quran very easy, and helped me learn many different stories and rulings from the daily books time when we learn either fiqh or Seerah. This benefited me greatly because I knew what to do and how the prophet did it on different occasions. The madrassah environment is great overall and I was also able to make a bunch of great friends there. - Hafiz Yusuf Syed, 13 years old

"I have memorized most of my Quraan in this madrassah. Ever since I joined, My Quran became better and I was able to perform to my full potential.
