Our Programs

Full Time Hifz
  • Our main and longest running program
  • Classes occur Monday-Friday
  • Timings: Monday-Thursday = 8:30am-3:00pm
  • Friday's = 8:30am-12:45pm (Timings will change due to Daylight Savings Time ending)
  • Breaks are given for Snacks, Lunch, and Zuhur Salah
  • Students are provided a positive learning environment throughout the day
  • Subjects include(in addition to hifz): Fiqh, Akhlaaq, Tajweed, Seerah, Aqaaid, Ahadith
  • Students are taught in a practical manner to ensure application of knowledge
  • Students are also scheduled a Field Trip!
Evening Hifz
  • Program constructed for students that wish to memorize the Glorious Qur'aan while taking care of other responsibilities
  • Classes occur Monday-Friday
  • Timings: Monday-Thursday:3:45pm-7:00pm
  • Friday: 5:00pm-7:00pm
  • No classes during Ramadan
Summer Hifz
  • Program constructed for students that wish to memorize the Glorious Qur'aan during the summer holidays
  • Program begins from July and concludes end of August
  • Classes occur Monday-Friday
  • Timings: Monday-Thursday = 8:30am-3:00pm, Friday's = 8:30am-12:45pm
  • Breaks are given for Snacks, Lunch, and Dhuhr Salah
  • Students are provided a positive learning environment throughout the day
  • Subjects include(in addition to hifz): Fiqh, Akhlaaq, Tajweed, Seerah, Aqaaid
  • Students are taught in a practical manner to ensure application of knowledge
  • Students are also scheduled a Field Trip!
