Student Resources

  1. Read Naazirah 10 times.
  2. Read first ayah with looking at least 5 times, then test yourself.
  3. Read second ayah with looking at least 5 times,then test yourself.
  4. If 5 times is not enough for you, then read the ayah more.
  5. Read both ayahs without looking, until they can be read without mistakes.
  6. Repeat steps for all of Sabaq.
  7. Remember to continuously test yourself on your whole sabaq!
  1. Read naazirah of first sabaq.
  2. Try to read first sabaq without looking.
  3. If any mistakes, keep repeating naazirah until you can read it without mistakes.
  4. Repeat procedure for second sabaq.
  5. Recite both sabaq's without looking inside.
  6. Repeat process for whole sabaq para!

  2. Try to read first ruku/page without looking.
  3. If any mistakes, keep repeating naazirah until you can read it without mistakes.
  4. Repeat procedure for second ruku/page.
  5. Recite both rukus/pages without looking inside.
  6. Repeat process for whole quarter
