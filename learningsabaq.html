How To Learn Sabaq

Introduction
Instructions

Step 1: Open your sabaq of the day in your Quran and mark exactly where the starting and ending point of your sabaq are. (Below is the 3rd page of the Quran and ½ of the page is marked to memorize).

Step 2: Do naazirah of your entire sabaq of the day 10 times from start to finish, make sure to be reading loudly, clearly and as fluently as possible. Due to it being sabaq, this portion will be hard to read for non native Arabic speakers, doing naazirah 10 times will help make reading easier, more fluent, and will also allow the student to already have parts of it already somewhat memorized due to the repetition.

Step 3: Mark of a small portion of the sabaq in your mind (Don’t physically mark anything), limiting it to 1 small ayah or ½ of a big ayah. Start repeating this small portion until reading without looking is possible. (Below is the first full small ayah highlighted).

Step 4: Mark another small portion of the sabaq, limiting it to 1 small ayah or ½ of a big ayah. Start repeating this small portion until reading without looking is possible. (Below is a big ayah cut into half).

Step 5: Repeat this process for all mistakes until full ruku is done. Then read the entire ruku without looking 3 times until there are no stucks or mistakes.

Step 6: Repeat this process for the next ruku.

Step 7: Read the first ruku and second ruku looking inside once and then once again without looking. If mistakes or stucks occur, repeat both rukus until fluent.

Step 8: Learn the third ruku following steps 2-5. Then Read all 3 rukus looking inside once and then once again without looking. If mistakes or stucks occur, repeat all rukus until fluent.
